Photographers views : A 4 month struggle against the labour law

We are seventeen commited photographers with different status, motivations and skills.

We met during protests against the recent labor reform and we felt like gathering around this common project since all of us evolved with this project.

Because our photos shall not stay on our computers, facebook pages and websites, but should definitely return to all actors of this movement, we decided to organize this collective exhibition.

After the collective exhibition at the Bourse du Travail in Paris in September '16 with 30 photographers, some of our photos will be presented at La Bobine in Grenoble from January 9th til February 6th.



All practical informations are also available on Facebook.



We also need help to finance the whole project, so we have a crowdfunding on leetchi
